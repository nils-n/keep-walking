Our approach

Connect your Garmin Smartwatch to our website, and we will analyse the progression of your Body-Mass-Index (BMI) and daily steps. Add your emotional rating on your dashboard, and receive our feedback how you are progressing towards reaching or maintaining a healthy BMI and happiness in your life.

Slow, but Constant

We don't use a receipe such as “lose 10 kg in 2 weeks” - usually those are followed by increase in weight in same time, paired with emotional downs.

Be Happy - Stay Happy

Keeping track of your BMI and see your constant improvements will help you stay motivated.

Power of Science

Our approach is based on latest research in peer-reviewed journals - leverage the power of knowledge.

Your Journey

The secret to maintaining 7000 daily steps is creating the right habits. We will guide to to leverage the power of your Garmin Smartwatch to helping you maintain a healthy BMI and stay fit.

  1. 1. Sign Up

    Get access to your main activitiy board, enter your height and current weight. This is important to calculate your BMI (Body-mass-index).

  2. 2. Sync with Your Garmin Watch

    From your personal page, sync with your Garmin Watch using Garmin Connect. Don't worry, we don't save your login details.

  3. 3. Start Walking and keep track

    Receive a first summary of your last month based on your walks. We will guide your carfully to reach and maintain your healthy BMI. Log in as often as you can, and track your progression.
